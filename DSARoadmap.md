## Daily Study Protocol (The 15-Minute Review)

At the end of every study session, spend **15 minutes** doing the following:

1.  **The "Why" Test:** Can you explain why a specific data structure was chosen over another for the problems solved today?
2.  **Pattern Tagging:** Don't remember the problem; remember the pattern. (e.g., "This was a Sliding Window problem because it asked for a contiguous subarray.")
3.  **Complexity Audit:** Re-calculate the Time and Space complexity for every solution you wrote without looking at notes.
4.  **Dry Run:** Mentally (or on paper) trace one edge case (null input, single element, large values) through your code.
